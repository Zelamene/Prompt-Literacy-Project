# Experiment: Creativity and Idea Generation

## Objective

Explore how adding context, constraints, and formatting instructions influences AI-generated ideas for a creative task.

## Prompt Progression

### Initial Prompt

> Give me business ideas.

### Revised Prompt

> Give me five tech business ideas.

### Improved Prompt

> Give me five tech business ideas for South African university students. Each idea should address a real campus problem, be feasible with minimal funding, and include a one-sentence description.

### Final Prompt

> You are a start-up advisor working with university students in South Africa. Generate five tech business ideas that:
>
> * Address a real challenge faced by students, such as transport, food, study resources, mental health, or part-time work.
> * Can be tested with a budget of R5,000 or less.
> * Do not require advanced coding skills.
>
> For each idea, provide:
>
> * A name
> * A one-sentence problem statement
> * A one-sentence solution
> * The most important assumption to test first
>
> Present the results in a table.

## Analysis

The initial prompt provides very little guidance, so the response may include a broad range of business ideas with varying levels of relevance. As additional information is included, the prompt becomes more focused by specifying:

* the target users (South African university students);
* the type of problem to address;
* practical constraints such as budget and technical complexity; and
* a consistent output format.

These additions encourage responses that are more closely aligned with the intended purpose and make the ideas easier to compare and evaluate.

## Key Findings

This experiment demonstrates that adding context and practical constraints can help focus AI-generated ideas on a specific audience or problem. Clearly defining the expected format also produces responses that are more organised and easier to review.

## Takeaway

Creative prompts can often benefit from clear objectives, realistic constraints, and a defined output format. These elements help guide the AI toward responses that are more relevant to the intended task.

## Survey Connection

Several participants in the EMZAMS AI survey reported using AI to generate ideas or support brainstorming. This experiment illustrates how providing additional context and clear requirements can help produce suggestions that are more closely aligned with a specific goal.
